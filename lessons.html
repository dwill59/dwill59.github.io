‘Pay As You Learn’ Driving Lessons £22 per hour or £205 for 10

You can always pay for your driving lessons on a Pay-As -You-Go basis. This is the most popular and flexible way to book and pay for your driving lessons. Lesson times and lesson pick up/drop off points are fully flexible to suit you, so why not learn to drive on the way to college or work for example?

Lesson duration is also fully flexible to optimise your learning, you can book one hour, two hour or even one and a half hour time slots, all charged at the same hourly rate. No need to pay large sums of money up front.

Pass+ Plus Course £150

Passed your driving test? Why not hone your driving skills and save money on your car insurance with our Pass+ Plus Course. Pass Plus was introduced by the Driving Standards Agency to improve new driver’s skills and reduce the amount of accidents that new drivers are involved in. The course consists of 6 different modules covering night driving, motorways, dual carriageways, all weather driving, country roads and town driving. There is NO test at the end, the training is signed off by the instructor and sent to the DSA who will send you a Pass+ Plus certificate.

Car insurance can be expensive, especially for young people. Take advantage of the Pass+ Plus course and not only improve your driving skills, but save money on your car insurance too! Most insurers will give a discount of up to 40% for people who have completed the course.

The Pass+ Plus course can be completed in just one day (6 hours).

Eco-Safe Driving Course £49

With the price if fuel currently running at around £6 a gallon, increasing the amount of miles travelled per gallon has become high on many people’s agenda.

Wouldn’t it be great if you could cut your fuel bills by around 18% by applying a series of simple techniques whilst also improving safety and prolonging the life of your vehicle? Does this sound too good to be true? Very few people drive in a way that fully utilises the advances in modern engineering to the full. I can, in a easy easy to understand way, maximise your mpg whilst often also reducing journey times (within the law of course).

This 2-hour course starts with a short in car presentation followed by two identical drives of around 35 to 40 minutes each. After I analyse your first drive, I’ll explain how you can make savings. During your second drive you will be fully coached in the Eco-safe methods and afterwards you will be shown the improvement made on the car’s on board computer.

Refresher/Advanced Driving Lessons £22 per hour

Refresher/advanced driving lessons are available to any qualified driver who wants to improve their driving skills. These lessons can be especially helpful for drivers who have taken a brake from driving, or have passed their driving test but haven’t driven for a while. During these lessons I focus on building your confidence. If you are a nervous driver, I can help you overcome your driving fears.

When starting refresher/advanced lessons, we will discuss exactly what you would like to achieve and set out a course of lessons to achieve those goals. Lessons focus on safety, anticipation/planning and a smooth progressive drive.

There are many reasons why I get asked to help qualified drivers improve their skills, like: Confidence Building, Driving Phobias,. Manoeuvering, Country Roads, Motorways, Parking (like multi storey car parks) and Night Driving for example.

I can help any driver be more skilled, more confident and safer on the road.

Introduction to Driving in the UK £49

This 2 hour course is especially designed to help foreign licence holders get used to driving in the UK. Driving on the left is new to most foreign license holders and the UK will also have many unfamiliar rules of the road. This course takes two hours during which rules and common pitfalls will be explained and practical experience is gained in a safe and controlled environment.

I know that driving in a different country can be a challenge - my wife is dutch and we both regularly drive on the continent in both right hand and left hand drive vehicles, so I am very aware of many of the difficulties faced.

WANT TO GET A UK DRIVING LICENCE? - I have also helped many foreign licence holders on an international permit gain a full UK licence. I will be able to give you an idea of how close you are to UK test standard during the Introduction to Driving in the UK course, or you can book hourly driving lessons.
