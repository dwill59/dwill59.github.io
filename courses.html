Courses to meet your lifesytle

Our lifestyle courses are aimed at learners with a deadline and can work very well for some people. As everybody learns at their own rate, an assessment lesson is highly recommended. During the assessment I will look at how you learn and get an idea of how quickly you will progress. At the end of the assessment I will be able to judge which course will be best suited to your individual needs. This is charged at the normal hourly rate but is fully refundable if you go on to book a course.
